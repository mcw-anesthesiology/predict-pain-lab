Located on the scenic shore of Lake Michigan, Milwaukee offers an exceptional blend of stunning natural beauty and Midwest charm. A downtown RiverWalk connects German heritage-inspired Old World Third Street to the Historic Third Ward with its shops and restaurants.

We’re a city of colorful, walk-around neighborhoods, acclaimed culinary scene and entrepreneurial spirit, where exciting attractions like Discovery World and Milwaukee Art Museum meet brewery tours, excursion boats, professional sports and a summer-long schedule of lakefront festivals. You’ll find all this and more on tap in Milwaukee. Check out some of our Milwaukee favorites below.
